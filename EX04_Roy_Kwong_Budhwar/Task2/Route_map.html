How to get to IfGI by train and bus ?

After reaching Muenster Hauptbahnhof by train, you can take Bus No. 13 or 4 from Muenster Hauptbahnhof to Mendelstrasse busstop to go to IfGI at Heisenbergstrasse, 2, which is a Green and Black colour building right in front of the Mendelstrasse busstop.
